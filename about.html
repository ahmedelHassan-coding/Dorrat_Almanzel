We Are CleanQatar

Your Trusted Cleaning Partner Since 2018

CleanQatar was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services to homes and businesses across Qatar. What started as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into one of the most trusted cleaning companies in the region.

Our journey began when our founder recognized the need for reliable, high-quality cleaning services that prioritize customer satisfaction, environmental responsibility, and professional excellence.

Today, we are proud to serve thousands of satisfied clients throughout Qatar, from private residences to large commercial complexes. Our dedicated team of trained professionals is committed to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and customer service.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional cleaning services that exceed client expectations, enhance living and working environments, and contribute to healthier spaces across Qatar.

Our Vision

To become the premier cleaning service provider in Qatar, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and environmental responsibility.

Our Values

  • Integrity in all our dealings
  • Excellence in service delivery
  • Respect for our clients and staff
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Continuous improvement
