Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas in Qatar do you service?

We provide cleaning services throughout Doha and the surrounding areas, including West Bay, The Pearl, Al Waab, Lusail, Al Sadd, and other locations across Qatar. If you're unsure if we service your area, please contact us.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability. However, we understand that cleaning needs can arise unexpectedly, so we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning products that are effective yet safe for your family, pets, and the environment. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, please let us know, and we'll be happy to accommodate your needs.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It's not necessary for you to be present during the cleaning, but you will need to arrange access for our team. Many clients provide a key or have a building security system that allows access. We ensure all our staff are thoroughly vetted and trustworthy.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment apps. For regular clients, we also offer monthly invoicing options.
